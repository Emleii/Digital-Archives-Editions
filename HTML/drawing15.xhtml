Unnamed Starkweather Drawing 15

Colored pencil drawing of a man leaning against the car. The man is probably Starkweather himself. He's smoking a cigarette and has Starkweather's red hair and glasses, also sporting the James dean look. The car is green with a red interior and there's a blue sky behind them. On the bottom right corner is Charles Starkweather's signature in red pen. The back of the drawing is blank.
